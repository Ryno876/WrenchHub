What should we call it?

Pick a name that captures the marketplace vibe — car owners finding mechanics, competitive bidding, convenience

A

MechaniQ

A play on "mechanic" + "queue" — your repair is queued up, mechanics line up to compete. Modern, techy feel.

B

BidWrench

Straightforward — bidding + wrenches. Instantly communicates what the app does. Memorable and easy to spell.

C

AutoBid

Clean and professional. "Auto" for automotive, "Bid" for the marketplace. Simple, brandable, easy to remember.

D

WrenchHub

Hub where car owners and mechanics connect. Feels like a platform/marketplace. Friendly and approachable.

E

FixFleet

Action-oriented — get it fixed. "Fleet" hints at scale and a network of mechanics ready to go. Energetic and modern.
