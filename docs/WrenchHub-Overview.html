What Is WrenchHub?

WrenchHub is a two-sided marketplace that eliminates the hassle of finding affordable, reliable auto repair. Car owners post what they need and mechanics compete to offer the best price — or car owners browse verified local mechanics and reach out directly. No more calling around for quotes. No more guessing who to trust.

Two Ways to Connect

Mode 1

Job Bidding

Post a job describing what you need. Mechanics compete with sealed bids. Pick the best offer based on price, ratings, and reviews. Perfect when you want competitive pricing and multiple options.

Mode 2

Browse & Contact

Search for mechanics by specialty, location, and ratings. View their full profile and reach out directly via messaging. Perfect when you know what you need and want to find the right person fast.

Revenue Model

Mechanic Subscriptions

Free tier with limited bids. Pro tier ($29-49/mo) unlocks unlimited bids, featured placement, analytics, and early access to new jobs.

Advertising

Non-intrusive, relevant ads from auto parts stores, dealerships, and insurance companies shown to car owners.

Free for Car Owners

Always. No fees, no subscriptions, no paywalls. Posting jobs, browsing, and messaging are completely free.
