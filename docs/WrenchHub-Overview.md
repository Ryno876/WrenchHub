# WrenchHub

**The Uber for Car Repairs & Maintenance**

---

## What Is WrenchHub?

WrenchHub is a marketplace app that connects car owners with mechanics. Instead of calling around for quotes, car owners post what they need and mechanics compete to offer the best price. Car owners can also browse local mechanics and reach out directly.

Think of it as **Uber meets Angi (formerly Angie's List) — but specifically for auto repair and maintenance.**

---

## How It Works

### For Car Owners

1. **Post a Job** — Describe what your car needs (oil change, brake repair, check engine light, etc.). Upload photos or videos. Our AI helps you describe the problem if you're not sure what's wrong.
2. **Get Bids** — Local mechanics see your job and submit competitive bids with full price breakdowns (parts, labor, fees). Bids are sealed so mechanics can't see each other's prices.
3. **Pick Your Mechanic** — Compare bids side by side. See each mechanic's price, ratings, reviews, distance, certifications, and estimated completion time. Message them with questions before deciding.
4. **Get It Fixed** — Accept a bid, schedule the work, and leave a review when it's done.

**Or skip the job post entirely** — browse local mechanics by specialty, read reviews, and message them directly. Like Yelp, but with the ability to connect instantly.

**Always free for car owners.** No fees, no subscriptions, no catches.

### For Mechanics

1. **Create a Profile** — Showcase your shop or mobile service. Add photos, certifications, specialties, and your service area.
2. **Find Jobs** — Browse a feed of local repair and maintenance jobs filtered to your specialties and location.
3. **Submit Bids** — Compete on price and quality. Provide detailed quotes so car owners know exactly what they're paying for.
4. **Build Your Reputation** — Earn reviews and ratings. Get verified to stand out from the competition.

## What Makes WrenchHub Different

| Feature | Traditional Way | WrenchHub |
|---|---|---|
| Getting quotes | Call 5+ shops, explain the problem each time | Post once, get multiple bids |
| Comparing prices | No easy way to compare | Side-by-side bids with full breakdowns |

## Two Ways to Connect

### 1. Job Bidding (Reverse Auction)
Car owner posts a job → mechanics bid → car owner picks the best offer. Great when you want competitive pricing and multiple options.

### 2. Browse & Contact (Directory)
Car owner searches for mechanics by specialty, location, and ratings → reaches out directly via messaging. Great when you know what you need and want to find the right person fast.

---

## Mechanic Verification

Mechanics can operate at two levels:

- **Standard** — Any mechanic can sign up and start connecting with customers immediately.
- **Verified** — Mechanics who submit their business license, insurance, and certifications earn a Verified badge. Verified mechanics rank higher in search results and build trust faster.

---

## Revenue Model

- **Freemium for Mechanics** — Free tier allows limited bids per month. Pro tier ($29-49/month) unlocks unlimited bids, featured placement, analytics, and early access to new jobs.
- **Advertising** — Non-intrusive, relevant ads (auto parts, insurance, dealerships) shown to car owners.
- **Car owners are always free.**

## Mobile & Shop Mechanics

Car owners can choose:
- **Mobile mechanic** — they come to your home or office
- **Shop-based** — you bring your car to their location
- **No preference** — see all options

This gives WrenchHub an edge over traditional shop-only directories.
